Wine · Growth · AI Infrastructure Growth & data lead

Tulip Winery — turning a family winery into a data-driven growth machine

Challenge

A respected family winery with a decade of marketing history, but data scattered across Meta, Google Ads, GA4 and WooCommerce — no single source of truth tying ad spend to actual sales.

What I built

A central marketing-intelligence vault feeding AI agents (copy, campaigns, strategy), a local-first CRM with a Claude assistant and a learning objection knowledge base, and an automated daily dashboard that treats WooCommerce as the source of truth and reconciles it against every ad platform — rebuilt and published every morning.

Impact

Real ROAS instead of platform-reported guesses, AI-ready knowledge that turns strategy into campaigns in minutes, and a B2B sales motion built on top of clean, trustworthy data.

  • 398campaigns orchestrated since 2015
  • ~37Ksocial followers across FB & IG
  • 4→1data sources reconciled into one truth

Marketplaces · Product 0→1 · Emerging markets Founder & builder

ChambaLocal & ColectiVa — 0→1 products for real communities in Oaxaca

Challenge

On Mexico's Oaxaca coast, workers and employers had no reliable way to find each other, and shared-transport (colectivo) riders had no live information — both problems where trust, not technology, is the real barrier.

What I built

ChambaLocal: a Spanish-first job marketplace as a Telegram bot and a Next.js + Supabase PWA, with double opt-in matching and anti-spam built in. ColectiVa: a transport app launched with a deliberate "Trojan-horse" strategy — a purely positive v1 that earns operator trust before adding the harder features.

Impact

Products designed around adoption and trust, not just features — security-first engineering, real maps, and a sequencing strategy that gets a foot in the door where blunt disruption would fail.

Fintech · BD Engine · Data Business development

Crypto B2B — an outbound engine that knows who's worth talking to

Challenge

B2B in crypto means finding the protocols that actually matter — by TVL, activity and traction — instead of spraying outreach.

What I built

A Python pipeline that pulls and enriches protocol data from open ecosystem APIs (DeFiLlama and others), scoring and prioritizing targets so business development starts with signal, not noise.

Impact

A repeatable, data-driven prospecting motion — the same discipline I bring to any market: research first, then sell.
